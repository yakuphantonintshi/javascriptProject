About

Blossom company was launched in the year 2008, where me and my teammates noticed the importance of flowers and how they can bring change in our world. Flowers give life to the soil, they add color and texture in our homes, they add biodiversity to gardens and environments. Flowers they help us to structure for plants and they are an essential food source for many organisms. Blossom company noticed that people are dying with anxiety and stress, so we decided to open up this flower business as the presence of vibrant and playful flowers can cause happy emotions and instantly affect our mood, hence they are given when one is sick. Feelings can be expressed through flowers. Allow us to heal you with our fresh flowers!
